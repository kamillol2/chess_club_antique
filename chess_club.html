Chess Club at Antique Coffee Place

ناد الفرس الذهبي للشطرنج

Since 1974, our club has welcomed generations of talented individuals from diverse backgrounds—Muslims, Jews, Christians, state officials, and civilians—all united by their passion for chess and love for the country.

Code of Conduct

Everyone is welcomed as long as you respect the following rules :

  1. Keep noise levels down.
  2. No music allowed.
  3. No food or drinks near the chess boards.
  4. Respect fellow members and opponents.
  5. Be kind and respectful to the Staff.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can anyone join the club?

Yes, everyone is welcome to join our chess club!

Can play even without a membership ?

Yes, A membership is not required.

What if I don't know how to play ?

You are still welcomed to come and learn.

Can I come any time I want ?

As long as it's within Antique's Work Hours.

Is there any tournements I can participate in ?

We host a local tournament every Sunday; Check out the WhatsApp Group link for more information down below.
